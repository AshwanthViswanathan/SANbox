# Author Notes

This lesson should help a child understand:

- habitats are places where living things find food, shelter, and safety
- the ocean has different habitats
- different ocean animals fit different habitats
- near-shore, reef, and deep-ocean habitats feel different

Keep the tone imaginative but concrete. Use fish, crabs, reefs, and deep-sea animals.

```teachbox-step
{
  "step_id": "intro-1",
  "type": "narration",
  "title": "What a habitat is",
  "script_chunks": [
    "Today we are learning about ocean habitats.",
    "A habitat is a place where a living thing can find food, shelter, and safety.",
    "The ocean has many kinds of habitats, not just one big same-looking place."
  ]
}
```
