title: Can Plants Grow Without Sunlight?
grade_band: 3-5
topic: biology
---

## explanation
Plants use sunlight to make food in their leaves. Without enough light, many plants become weak, pale, and slow to grow.

## prompts
- What do you think a plant needs every day?
- Have you seen a plant lean toward a window?

## checkpoints
- Student can name sunlight as a plant need
- Student can describe how low light changes plant growth

## questions
- Why do plants need sunlight?
- What else do plants need besides light?

## fallback
If the child is unsure, compare a plant to a solar-powered toy that only works when it gets enough energy.
