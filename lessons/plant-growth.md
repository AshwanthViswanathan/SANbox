# Author Notes

This lesson should help a child understand:

- plants use sunlight to make food
- leaves help plants capture light
- plants without enough sunlight often grow weak and pale
- some plants can survive a little while in low light, but they do not thrive

Keep the lesson concrete. Use windowsill plants, gardens, and simple observations.
