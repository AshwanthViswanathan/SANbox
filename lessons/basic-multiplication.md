## explanation
Multiplication is a faster way to add equal groups. If there are three groups with two stars in each group, that is the same as two plus two plus two.

## prompts
- Can you make groups with blocks or crayons?
- What does three groups of two look like?

## checkpoints
- Student can explain multiplication as equal groups
- Student can connect multiplication to repeated addition

## questions
- What does 4 times 3 mean?
- How can a picture help with multiplication?

## fallback
If the child needs support, draw circles around small groups of objects and count the groups together.
